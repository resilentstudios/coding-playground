Childrens Biscuits

Quick and easy bscuit that little ones love to make. We call them 1,2,3,4 biscuits and this recipe has been halved. (Originally 100g, 200g etc.)

Ingredients

  • 50g Sugar
  • 100g Butter
  • 150g Flour
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Essence

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to gas mark 4
  2. In a bowl add the sugar and butter and cream until light and fluffy.
  3. Add the vanilla essense and mix thoroughly.
  4. Add the sifted flour bit by bit and mix until it combines into a dough.
  5. Roll the dough out and cut into shapes.
  6. Place on a baking sheet and bake for 10 minutes
